The BEST Freddo Espresso Recipe!

Description

Freddo Espresso's are made to be sipped slowly while talking to your good friends. It's a delcious shot of espresso whipped fast with fresh ice and sweetened to taste. Once you try one, you'll be obsessed! Enough talking, lets get into making one.

Freddo Espresso Ingredients

  • Double Shot of Espresso
  • 5 Ice Cubes
  • Sweetener of Choice
  • A Powered Mixer
  • A glass cup and straw

Steps

  1. Pour your double shot of espresso into your powered mixer with 2 ice cubes
  2. Add your sweetener of choice to the mixer with the ice cubes
  3. Turn on the mixer and thoroughly mix the espresso with the ice cubes until it creates a rich looking frothe, turn off mixer
  4. Add the remaining 3 ice cubes into the glass cup
  5. Pour the freshly frothed Espresso atop the iced glass, add straw
  6. Sip slowly, while gossiping about the neighbourhood!
